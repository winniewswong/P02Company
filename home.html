Our Story

It’s hard to say if Great Wall Tea Company is better known for its must-see mosaic wall of tea tins, or for its varieties of fresh, quality loose-leaf teas. Owners Lauren Bowler and Sean Smith were inspired to open their modern tea bar by their love of tea and travel. They hope to foster conversation, community and even social responsibility around the tea bar. And don’t forget to ask for a sniff of any tea varieties that interest you.
